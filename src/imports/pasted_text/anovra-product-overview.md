# Anovra
### Radiant Skin Through Innovation

## Product Overview

Anovra is an AI-powered skincare intelligence and commerce platform built specifically for Africa. It helps individuals understand their skin through advanced image and video analysis while connecting them with trusted skincare vendors and personalized product recommendations.

Unlike traditional skincare marketplaces where customers purchase products based on assumptions, recommendations from friends, or social media trends, Anovra enables users to make informed skincare decisions using artificial intelligence.

The platform bridges the gap between customers seeking effective skincare solutions and verified skincare vendors by ensuring that product recommendations are tailored to each individual's unique skin condition.

Anovra is designed to improve skincare outcomes, reduce incorrect product usage, increase customer confidence, and help skincare businesses scale through intelligent technology.

# Our Solution

Anovra combines Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Image Analysis, and Personalized Recommendation Systems to analyze a user's skin and recommend products that best suit their skin condition.

Rather than recommending products based on trends, the platform makes recommendations based on what it detects from the user's skin.

The platform also connects users directly with trusted skincare vendors who already stock the recommended products.

# How Anovra Works

## For Individual Users

Individuals can visit Anovra without belonging to any skincare vendor.

The process is simple.

### Step 1
The user selects the skin area they want analyzed.

Examples include:

- Face
- Hands
- Legs
- Neck
- Back
- Any visible skin area

### Step 2

The user uploads:

- High-quality images
or
- Short videos

The platform provides guidance to ensure the images are clear enough for accurate analysis.

### Step 3

The AI engine analyzes the uploaded media.

The system evaluates visible characteristics such as:

- Skin texture
- Pigmentation
- Acne severity
- Pores
- Wrinkles
- Fine lines
- Redness
- Dryness
- Oiliness
- Skin tone consistency
- Visible inflammation
- Blemishes

### Step 4

Anovra generates a detailed skin report.

The report may include:

- Skin type
- Skin condition
- Possible skin concerns detected
- Overall skin health score
- Severity indicators
- Personalized recommendations

### Step 5

The recommendation engine searches products from verified vendors across the platform.

Users receive products specifically matched to their skin concerns.

Each recommendation includes:

- Product name
- Product image
- Key ingredients
- Product benefits
- Suitable skin type
- Usage instructions
- Possible precautions
- Expected results
- Vendor information
- Pricing
- Availability

### Step 6

Users can filter recommendations by:

- Country
- State
- City
- Vendor
- Product category

### Step 7

The user purchases directly from their preferred vendor.

# For Customers Visiting Through a Vendor

Anovra provides every skincare vendor with a unique analysis link.

The vendor simply shares this link through:

- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Facebook
- TikTok
- SMS
- Email
- Their website

When customers use this unique link:

The AI performs the same skin analysis.

However, instead of recommending products from every vendor on the platform, Anovra only recommends products available inside that specific vendor's mini-store.

This increases conversion rates while giving customers confidence that every recommendation is personalized.

---

# Vendor Verification Option

After receiving their AI-generated skin report, customers have two options.

### Option 1

Proceed immediately with the AI-generated recommendations and purchase products.

### Option 2

Share the skin analysis report with the vendor for additional professional review before making a purchase.

This allows vendors to:

- Validate recommendations
- Add personalized advice
- Build stronger customer relationships
- Increase customer trust

# Embedded Website Integration
